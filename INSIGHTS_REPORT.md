## Executive Summary

FY2024 was a strong performance year for the business. Total revenue reached **$800,466** across 2,000 orders, delivering a net profit of **$331,957** and an overall margin of **41.5%** — a figure that comfortably outperforms the technology retail industry average of approximately 38%.

The business shows healthy diversification across product categories and sales channels, with clear leaders that present both strengths to protect and opportunities to exploit. This report details the findings and provides five actionable recommendations for FY2025 growth planning.

## 8. Strategic Recommendations

### Recommendation 1 — Protect & Grow the Electronics Core
> **Priority: HIGH | Timeline: Q1 FY2025**

Electronics account for 74% of revenue, with Laptop Pro X1 alone at 33%. Conduct a supply chain risk audit for the top 3 products. Simultaneously, invest in marketing the next-tier products (Webcam, Portable SSD) to reduce single-product revenue concentration below 25%.

### Recommendation 2 — Launch a Q2 Mid-Year Sales Campaign
> **Priority: HIGH | Timeline: April–May FY2025**

Q2 is consistently the weakest quarter (25.4% of annual revenue). A targeted April–May campaign — combining bundle discounts on Electronics + Accessories and a dedicated Furniture push — could realistically recover $15–25K in "lost" Q2 revenue. Focus on Online channel promotions with Retail activation events.

### Recommendation 3 — Invest in the Central Region
> **Priority: MEDIUM | Timeline: H1 FY2025**

Central generates only $116K vs. North's $215K. Assign one additional sales rep to Central, run a region-specific digital marketing experiment (Google Ads, LinkedIn), and set a Q4 FY2025 target of $160K — a 37% growth target that would add approximately $43K to total revenue.

### Recommendation 4 — Build an Accessories Bundle Strategy
> **Priority: MEDIUM | Timeline: Q2 FY2025**

The Accessories category has strong margin parity with Electronics but only 19.8% revenue share. Introduce "Power Bundles" (Laptop + USB Hub + Keyboard, Webcam + Desk Lamp) with a 5–8% bundle discount. Bundle promotions typically increase AOV by 15–20% and reduce per-item discount pressure.

### Recommendation 5 — Implement Rep Performance Coaching Program
> **Priority: LOW–MEDIUM | Timeline: Q1–Q2 FY2025**

The 33% revenue gap between top and bottom reps represents structural underperformance. Formalize a monthly coaching cadence using Eva R. and David L. as peer mentors. If the bottom 2 reps improve to the team average ($100K), that recovers approximately $22K in annual revenue with zero new headcount.

## 10. Data Notes & Methodology

- **Dataset:** 2,000 synthetic transaction records, January–December 2024
- **Products:** 15 SKUs across Electronics, Accessories, Furniture, Stationery

- **Channels:** Online (55%), Retail (30%), Partner (15%)

- **Discounts:** Applied at 0%, 5%, 10%, 15%, 20% with market-realistic frequency
- **Data quality:** Zero missing values; dates validated; no duplicate Order IDs
